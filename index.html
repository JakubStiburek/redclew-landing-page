Seamless Client Intake

Client onboarding, simplified. Collect just an email address, and RedClew handles the rest. Your new clients complete their paperwork, provide essential information, and review your policies—all before their first session. Start therapy, not paperwork.

Intelligent Scheduling

Put scheduling on autopilot. Set your availability once, and let clients book, reschedule, and manage their own appointments. No more back-and-forth emails or missed connections. Your calendar stays organized while you focus on what matters.

Hassle-free Payments

Get paid on time, every time. RedClew's payment system sends automated reminders, processes payments, and handles sliding-scale arrangements. Manage your finances with confidence while maintaining the personal touch your practice deserves.
